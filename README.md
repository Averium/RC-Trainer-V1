# RC-Trainer-V1

This project is a legal sized trainer RC aircraft design

# Specifications:

- Mass:         181 g
- Length:       520 mm
- Wingspawn:    708 mm
- CG:           157 mm

- Powerplant:   1 x EMAX MT1806 2280 kv  &  1 x 600 mah LIPO
              
- Channels:     4 Channel
                  - Throttle
                  - Elevator
                  - Rudder
                  - Ailerons
                
- Materials:
  - 5 mm foam board sheet
  - 3 mm plywood sheet
  - 4 mm BBQ stick
  - 12 x 12 mm balsa slat
  - 1 mm spring steel wire
  - 3D printed ABS plastic
