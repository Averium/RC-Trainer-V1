1-22-3(-4)-NAME

1 - Type/material
	- PF: foam part
	- PW: wood part
	- PP: plastic part
	- PM: metal part
	- C: commertial
	- A: assembly

2 - Part number
	- from zero to N < 100, unique for each material/assembly

3 - Orientation
	- L: left
	- R: right
	- 0: symmetrical

(4) - Production state (only for parts)
	- 0: first state
	- n: n-th state

NAME - short name of the part (only in folder names)
